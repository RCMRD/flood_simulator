The flood simulator tool integrates Kenya Department of Water Resources (KDWR) flood-rating curves and CREST-modeled streamflow with a higher-resolution digital elevation model dataset to generate flood inundation maps. The stand-alone version of this capability that enables users to incorporate streamflow inputs from sources other than CREST also sends the inundation map and relevant information regarding a flagged location to appropriate ministry personnel. SERVIR-Eastern and Southern Africa will use CREST model products to generate real-time and short-term forecast flood inundation maps for KDWR-defined gauging locations.
